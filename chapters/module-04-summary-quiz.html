Demand & Supply Shifters

Review curve shifts, movements along curves, and the four-step equilibrium process.

Scope of Practice Quiz

This checkpoint focuses on the core Module 4 skills. Use it to test whether you can distinguish movements from shifts, identify demand and supply shifters, and predict how equilibrium changes.

A movement along a curve is caused by the good's own price. A shift of a curve is caused by another factor, such as income, tastes, technology, input prices, regulation, expectations, or the number of buyers or sellers.

Use the four-step process to identify the market, determine which curve shifts, move the curve in the correct direction, and compare the new equilibrium with the old one.
